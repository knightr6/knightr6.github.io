Build a Haiku Web App


Your Haiku will be presented here.

The Haiku format is three lines.

The syllabic structure is:

Line One_ : 5 syllables
Line Two_ : 7 syllables
Line Three : 5 syllables

Though English and few other languages are as syllabic in structure as Japanese; Build a Haiku Web App is designed to follow the - 3 line - 5 - 7 - 5 - syllabic structure rigorously.
